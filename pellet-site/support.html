Frequently asked questions

How do I add a pellet delivery or refill?
Open the app and tap the add button on your dashboard. Select delivery or refill and save the entry.

Can I share data with my household?
Yes. The Pro upgrade enables household sharing through iCloud. Invite family members from the Household settings screen.

How do I restore purchases?
Go to Settings in the app and select “Restore Purchases.” Your Pro status will be reactivated if available.

Are my notifications customizable?
You can set custom thresholds and notification preferences in Settings.
